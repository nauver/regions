Personal data, image and testimony disclaimer

This notice is a practical event notice based on GDPR principles. It should be reviewed by the relevant Data Protection Officer or legal service before production use.

1. Purpose of the activity

The Proud of My Region activity collects short participant contributions to display regional diversity during an event. The submitted information may be shown on the public wall, on the dedicated map page, and on internal event screens.

2. Data processed

  • First name and last name entered by the participant.
  • Selected region, country, NUTS code and approximate geographic coordinates of the selected region.
  • Short testimony entered by the participant.
  • Photo taken through the kiosk, if provided.
  • Consent metadata, including consent checkbox status, consent timestamp and disclaimer version.

3. Legal basis and consent

The activity relies on the participant's explicit affirmative action through a mandatory unticked checkbox. Consent is requested for the specific purpose of collecting, moderating and displaying the submitted information as part of the activity.

4. Publication and audience

Approved submissions may be visible on public-facing screens and pages connected to the activity. Moderators may approve, hide or delete submissions from the administration page.

5. Withdrawal and deletion

Participants may request withdrawal of consent and deletion of their contribution. Withdrawal does not affect processing that took place before the withdrawal request.

6. Storage and retention

Data is stored in the configured Supabase project and images are stored in the configured storage bucket. The organiser should define and apply a retention period appropriate to the event.

7. Participant rights

Subject to applicable data protection rules, participants may have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and withdrawal of consent.

8. No automated profiling

The activity does not perform automated decision-making or profiling. Moderation is manual and limited to approving, hiding or deleting submissions.

9. Third-party services

The activity uses GitHub Pages, Supabase, Leaflet/OpenStreetMap, and Eurostat/GISCO NUTS data.

10. Credits

Map tiles/data: © OpenStreetMap contributors. Regional/NUTS data: © European Commission – Eurostat GISCO and © EuroGeographics for administrative boundaries.
